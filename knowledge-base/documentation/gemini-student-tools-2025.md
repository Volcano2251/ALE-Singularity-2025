# Gemini Student Tools (August 2025)

**Source:** https://blog.google/products/gemini/new-gemini-tools-students-august-2025/

## Executive Summary
In August 2025, Google introduced a suite of new Gemini features specifically designed for students to act as an AI-powered "learning companion." These tools focus on deep understanding rather than just quick answers, offering step-by-step guidance, integrated visual aids, and exam preparation capabilities. Additionally, a free 1-year subscription to the **Google AI Pro Plan** is being offered to eligible students in select countries.

## Key Takeaways

### New Learning Features
These features are available to all Gemini users:
1.  **Guided Learning:**
    *   **Function:** Breaks down complex problems into step-by-step explanations.
    *   **Goal:** Teaches the "how" and "why" behind a concept rather than just providing the final answer.
    *   **Capability:** Can generate personalized study guides from uploaded course files.
2.  **Integrated Visuals:**
    *   **Function:** Automatically enriches responses with high-quality diagrams, images, and YouTube videos.
    *   **Use Case:** ideal for visualizing biological processes (e.g., photosynthesis), cellular structures, or historical events.
3.  **Exam Prep Tools:**
    *   **Function:** Instantly converts class notes or quiz results into flashcards and structured study guides.
    *   **Benefit:** Reinforces key concepts through active recall.

### Google AI Pro Plan Offer
Eligible students in the **US, Japan, Indonesia, Korea, and Brazil** can claim a **free 1-year subscription**.
*   **Plan Includes:**
    *   **Gemini 2.5 Pro:** Advanced model for faster reasoning and larger context windows (uploading images/files).
    *   **Deep Research:** Generates personalized reports sourced from hundreds of websites.
    *   **NotebookLM:** An AI research assistant with 5x more audio/video overviews.
    *   **Veo 3:** Generates 8-second videos from text or image prompts.
    *   **Jules:** An asynchronous AI coding agent for debugging and feature building.
    *   **2 TB Storage:** Expanded Google Drive space.

### How to Use for Study
*   **Concept Deep Dives:** Upload course PDFs and ask Gemini to explain specific sections using the "Guided Learning" framework.
*   **Visual Aid:** When studying anatomy or geography, ask questions like *"Explain the structure of a plant cell"* to see the integrated diagrams.
*   **Active Recall:** Upload a past quiz and prompt: *"Create a set of 10 flashcards based on the mistakes I made in this quiz."*
