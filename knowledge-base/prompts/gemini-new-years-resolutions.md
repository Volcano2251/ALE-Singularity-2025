# 10 Gemini Prompts for New Year's Resolutions

**Source:** https://blog.google/products/gemini/google-gemini-prompts-new-years-resolutions/

## Executive Summary
This article outlines 10 specific, actionable prompts to help users set, plan, and keep their New Year's resolutions using Gemini. It emphasizes that a lack of planning is a major failure point and demonstrates how Gemini can act as a personal coach, scheduler, and analyst to bridge the gap between intent and action. The prompts cover health, career, education, and finance.

## Key Takeaways
*   **Actionable Planning:** The core value of Gemini here is converting vague goals (e.g., "get fit") into structured, day-by-day plans.
*   **Multimodal Capabilities:** Prompts leverage Gemini's ability to analyze spreadsheets (budgeting), process video/audio (interview prep), and create visual aids (flashcards).
*   **Integration:** The article highlights using Gemini Extensions (Google Workspace, Calendar) to schedule tasks directly.
*   **Gems:** Suggests creating custom "Gems" (personalized versions of Gemini) to act as dedicated accountability partners for specific goals.

## The Prompts

### 1. Fitness Training
> "I want to run the New York Marathon in 6 months. I recently completed a half-marathon in 2 hours and can train 5 days a week. Build a month-by-month training plan that helps me continue building endurance."

### 2. Home Organization
> "I want to declutter and clean my apartment this month. Create a 30-day plan that breaks the process down into simple daily tasks that take no more than 20 minutes, and doesn’t require buying any new supplies."

### 3. Language Learning
> "I’d like to learn basic French. Generate a 30-day guided study plan for the following topics, and create an interactive multiple-choice quiz on verb conjugations. Also generate a set of 50 flashcards for phrases."

### 4. Career Development (Interview Prep)
> "I have a job interview for a [role] next week. Simulate a live, conversational interview with me focusing on behavioral questions. Second, create a detailed feedback report after I upload a video rehearsal of myself, analyzing my speaking pace, body language and use of filler words."

### 5. Healthy Eating (Meal Planning)
> "I want to eat healthier. Every Sunday at 9 a.m., generate a 7-day, high-protein and vegetarian dinner plan. I have about 45 minutes to cook each night. Include a weekly shopping list."

### 6. Budgeting
> "Analyze the uploaded Google Sheet containing my monthly expenses for the last quarter, and identify 3 areas of non-essential spending. Suggest practical weekly budgeting rules I could implement immediately to save 15%."

### 7. Reading Goals
> "I want to read 12 business books in 2026. Set up a recurring action for the first of every month: suggest a list of 5 non-fiction books related to advertising and generate a sample monthly reading and review schedule."

### 8. Habit Replacement
> "I want to reduce my phone screen time by 2 hours a week. Outline a 'Habit Replacement Plan' that identifies my common trigger times and suggests a positive alternative activity for each."

### 9. Journaling (Writer's Block)
> "Give me 7 unique, one-sentence journal prompts for the week that encourage reflection on my goals and progress."

### 10. Long-term Maintenance
> "It is July 1st. I have successfully kept my resolution to [resolution name] for 6 months. Create a 3-part maintenance plan to help me turn this into a permanent habit and set a stretch goal for the remainder of 2026."
